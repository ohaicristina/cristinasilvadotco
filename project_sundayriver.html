Sunday River Mobile First Redesign Concept

Information Architecture, Wireframes (Live and Hand-drawn), Visual Design, Front-end Development

A mobile-first redesign concept for the Sunday River ski resort in Bethel, Maine. This was a final project for an Interactive Design class at Northeastern University. Keeping in mind the context in which the users were accessing the site (on the mountain vs. off the mountain), I restructured the site to have the most important information such as finding open trails and maps, purchasing tickets and viewing current conditions.
