Scriptstick Initial Landing Page

Visual & UI Design, Wireframes, Consulting

Designed an initial landing page for Scriptstick, a javascript based microcontroller now known as Tessel. The goal was to create a professional and smart landing page for possible investors as a well as software developers who would be interested in the product. Began with sketched wireframes and moved to high-fidelity mockups. Also acted as a consultant for the team for branding, their updated site and other design challenges.
